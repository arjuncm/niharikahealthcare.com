Welcome to Niharika Ayurvedic Health Care Center

Ayurveda, the science of life, is the oldest healthcare system in the world. More than 5,000 years ago, the great saints of India researched & studied the working of the human body& practiced a natural system of medicine. The science of Ayurveda has since been passed on from generation to generation. Unlike most of other forms of medicine, its approach to innumerable ailments is dispensed not only to the body but to the mind & soul as well. Ayurveda zeroes in not only symptoms of disease, but treats the individual. Thus it cares not just for an affected part, but for the entire body.
